# Explore Command

## Description

Explore new ideas and potential features â€” from raw concepts to partially-defined features.

## Trigger

User wants to explore a new idea or feature.

## Steps

1. **Understand the idea**: Clarify what's being explored (raw concept vs. feature).
2. **Break it down**: Identify components, dependencies, and unknowns.
3. **Research**: Use MCP tools (`gh_grep`, `fetch`) to find similar patterns/examples.
4. **Analyze feasibility**: Consider technical, design, and product constraints.
5. **Document findings**: Capture insights, trade-offs, and next steps.

## Output Format

- **Idea Summary**: What's being explored.
- **Context**: Current understanding and assumptions.
- **Exploration Areas**: Key questions or aspects to investigate.
- **Findings**: Discoveries, patterns, examples found.
- **Feasibility**: Technical/product considerations.
- **Next Steps**: Recommendations for further exploration or validation.
